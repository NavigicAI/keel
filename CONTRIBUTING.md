# Contributing

Keel is early, and public contributions should stay focused on public-safe
research and evaluation artifacts.

Useful contributions include:

- Literature review corrections or additions.
- Benchmark landscape updates.
- Public-safe synthetic evaluation cases.
- Scorer and rubric design notes.
- Reproducibility, citation, and documentation improvements.

Do not contribute raw personal data, production transcripts, customer documents,
private prompts, secrets, or unredacted traces.

Before opening a pull request:

1. Check that every source is public and cited.
2. Remove local paths, private organization details, and unpublished strategy.
3. Confirm datasets are synthetic, redacted, or explicitly approved for
   publication.
4. Explain what changed and why it matters for personal-agent evaluation.
